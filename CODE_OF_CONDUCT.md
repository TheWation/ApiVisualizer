# Contributor Code of Conduct

API Visualizer is intended to be a respectful and practical project space. By participating, you agree to help keep the community welcoming and useful.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and considerate of others.
2. Give constructive feedback and assume good intent.
3. Keep discussions focused on the project and the issue at hand.
4. Respect privacy and do not share private information without permission.
5. Be patient with contributors of different experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, insults, discriminatory language, personal attacks, and repeated disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior conflicts with this code of conduct.
